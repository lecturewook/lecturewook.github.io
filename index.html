Welcome!

I’m a researcher interested in how demographic changes, immigration shape political phenomena, and political representation. My work primarily focuses on American Politics, Race and Ethnic Politics (REP), and Public Policy, with special attention to immigrant representation, perceptions of immigrants, and cultural assimilation. I’ve completed my undergraduate studies in Political Science and obtained a Master’s degree in Public Policy. In my spare time, I enjoy reading, exercising, fishing, and engaging in volunteer activities. If you share similar interests or would like to discuss academic topics, please feel free to get in touch.
